Our Story

More than a restaurant - a community.

Jason Cooksey, the founder of Restaurante, has been involved with this restaurant for 5 years, and involved in the culinary arts for 23. Jason’s love for food comes from a young age. One day, Jason was visiting a friend, and at this friend’s house, their mother made them something to eat. The meal was mindblowing. It brought tears to his eyes!

From that moment, Jason knew that he wanted to know how to do that; he wanted to know how to move people with food. Growing up in Fresno, he stays true to his hometown and wants to make a change.

To do this, Jason decided to open a restaurant that is all about the community and using local ingredients. From the lettuce used in the burgers, to the beef that makes up the patties, everything is local, and everything is fresh. The team at Restaurante whole heartedly believe that eating local is the way to go and that this can help transform the community.

At Restaurante, we believe that a friendly environment is everything. To reinforce this, we do not have a bar. While we do still serve alcoholic beverages, we believe that part of a nice dining experience shouldn’t include sitting forward and staring at a wall of television. We believe that by doing this, we open up our customers to have a more positive, and connective experience.
